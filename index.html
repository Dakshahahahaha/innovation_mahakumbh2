About Innovation Mahakumbha

A platform for creativity, entrepreneurship, and academic–industry collaboration

Innovation Mahakumbha 2025 proudly showcases student-led innovative projects across SNDT Women’s University departments and affiliated colleges. This initiative fosters creativity, entrepreneurship, grassroots innovation, and academic–industry collaboration in alignment with the vision of a self-reliant India and the objectives of the PM-USHA Scheme.

In collaboration with the Wadhwani Foundation, over 200 faculty members received training in innovation, entrepreneurship, and Agentic AI. Guided by these mentors, students have developed technology-driven prototypes and sustainable solutions addressing challenges in healthcare, education, agriculture, energy, digital inclusion, and women’s empowerment.

With 500+ projects curated by 200+ mentors and 5,000+ visitors, the event promises to be a mega showcase of innovation, talent, and collaboration.
